Privacy Policy

プライバシーポリシー

1. Basic Policy

Japan Chef-in-Residence Empowerment Association (General Incorporated Association) (hereinafter referred to as "the Association") recognizes the importance of protecting personal information and has established this Privacy Policy to ensure the appropriate handling of personal information.

2. Definition of Personal Information

Personal information refers to information that can identify a specific individual, including name, address, phone number, email address, and other information that can identify a specific individual.

3. Purpose of Use

The Association uses personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To respond to inquiries
  • To send information about events and activities
  • For statistical analysis

4. Collection of Personal Information

The Association collects personal information through appropriate means and within the scope necessary to achieve the purpose of use.

5. Management of Personal Information

The Association takes necessary and appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, destruction, alteration, and leakage of personal information.

6. Provision to Third Parties

The Association will not provide personal information to third parties without the prior consent of the individual, except in cases permitted by law.

7. Disclosure and Correction

If an individual requests disclosure, correction, or deletion of their personal information, the Association will respond promptly after verifying the identity of the requester.

8. Use of Cookies

The Association may use cookies to improve the user experience on this website. Users can disable cookies through their browser settings.

9. Minors

For use by minors, we may request consent from a parent or guardian as necessary.

10. Amendments

If this Policy is amended, changes will be announced on this website, and important changes will be notified in an easy-to-understand manner.
